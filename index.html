The Cold One

By: Michael Echevarria

A heart once filled with love,
A person who would care,
Now he looks above,
As it all fades in the air.

Nothing affects him,
He lost his human feeling,
They try to hurt him,
While he's healing.

He's all alone,
Nothing left for him,
He's lost in his zone,
Blood looks good for a swim.
